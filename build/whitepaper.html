Christ Coin Whitepaper

Welcome to the official whitepaper of Christ Coin, the platform native token for our Web3 Christian Community. Below you'll find detailed information about our token, its purpose, features, and more.

Token Overview

Christ Coin (CHR) is a decentralized cryptocurrency built on the Ethereum blockchain. It serves as the primary currency for transactions within our community platform.

Key Features

  • Secure and transparent transactions
  • Built-in rewards system for community engagement
  • Supports charitable giving and donations
  • Tokenized assets and digital goods marketplace
  • Integrated with our Christian social network

Token Distribution

The total supply of Christ Coin is capped at 100 million tokens. Distribution is as follows:

  • 50% - Community rewards and incentives
  • 25% - Development and ecosystem growth
  • 15% - Charity fund for global causes
  • 10% - Team allocation and reserves
